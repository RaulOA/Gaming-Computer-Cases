About Us

GCC started in the garage of the owner, Raúl, in 2011. While Raúl created PC cases, his friend Isabel & Maria managed the office and scheduled sales. After a short time, the job was too much for one person to handle, so he hired more. Five years later, we have over 20 full-time designers at headquarters, a full-time office staff to keep designers on schedule, and our loyal customers well looked after. Regardless of the size or complexity of your project, we believe it's all about the relationship. Simply put, we want to be the company you turn to with the peace of mind knowing that we will always have your best interests at heart.

QUALITY

GCC stands behind the craftsmanship of every job we do. Our goal is to earn your lifetime purchase. We know that the only way we can earn your repeat purchase is to provide you with quality work and stand behind what we do.

SERVICE

We pride ourselves on exceeding our clients' expectations at every opportunity we get. We look for solutions to problems, not excuses. Our friendly staff is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Your emergency is our emergency.

VALUE

Our value is found in that we charge a fair price for quality work. Usually, when you find a deal, you get what you pay for and it shouldn't cost you an arm and a leg to get a chance at a quality case. We charge what we have to pay to bring you the highest level of craftsmanship without overpaying like some of our competitors do.
